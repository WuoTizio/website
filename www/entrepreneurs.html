Software technologies are more and more becoming a key aspect for enabling business success. Differently from other technologies, software cannot just be bought and used within your organization. Software requires to be continuously customized in order to be adpated to the business needs. Such an aspect requires a continuous investment approach in order to make software adaptation structurally part of your business.

Evolving software quickly and costly is now a strong requirement to be achieved. Software technologies can help you in increasing these aspects since they represent the concret tools used for building software. But which is the right technology to be used? There not exists a 'right' technology in absolute, but there exist the right technology at the right momentum. It happens the same with human languages, there is not a right language, but if you are travelling in Italy, maybe italian is the best language to know, if you are in France, french is the one.
